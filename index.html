Prem Ki Naiya - प्रेम की नैया

A devotional offering about seeing the divine everywhere and trusting in Lord Ram's grace.

This is a short devotional clip expressing the idea that the divine is present in the mountains, the views, and everywhere one looks, and that the boat of love is safely carried by Lord Ram.

Original Verse (Hindi)

मे में तू है, पहाड़ों में तू है, नजारों में तू है, देखूं जी भर में, वहां तू दिखाई दे। प्रेम की नैया है राम के भरोसे, प्रेम की नैया को पार लगा दो।

Meaning (English)

Verse Translation:

You are in me, in the mountains, in the views; wherever I look with a full heart, I see you. The boat of love rests in Ram's care, who carries it safely across.
